## Part 2: The Prompt Engineering Formula (10:10 AM – 10:35 AM)

*(Screen share your browser. Open a blank document or slide.)*

**Script:**

> *"Before we open any tool, we need to understand the secret language of AI.*
>
> *Most people fail with AI because they type: 'a dog.' The AI gives them a random, boring dog.*
>
> *But if you type: 'a golden retriever playing in the snow at sunset, cinematic lighting, 4K'—the AI gives you a masterpiece.*
>
> *The difference is **specificity**.*
>
> *Here is the 4-part formula for a perfect AI prompt:*
>
> **1. Subject** – What is the main object? *(e.g., 3D dinosaur)*
> **2. Style & Color** – How does it look? *(e.g., wearing sunglasses, blue and purple)*
> **3. Lighting & Environment** – Where is it? *(e.g., neon city background, cinematic lighting)*
> **4. Quality** – How clear is it? *(e.g., 4K, high resolution, photorealistic)*
>
> *Let’s combine them:*
>
> *"3D character of a cool blue dinosaur wearing sunglasses, neon city background, cinematic lighting, 4K."*
>
> *That is a perfect prompt. Memorize this formula. It will be the foundation of everything you create this year."*
