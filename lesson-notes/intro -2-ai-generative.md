(Screen share a slide or a simple document.)

Script:

"So, what exactly is Generative AI?

Imagine you have a super-smart assistant that has read everything on the internet. It has seen billions of images. It has read millions of books. It has listened to more music than you could ever hear.

Now, you ask it: 'Create a picture of a Lagos sunset with a futuristic car.'

Within 10 seconds, it creates a brand-new image that has never existed before.

That is Generative AI. It doesn't copy. It generates. It invents. It creates.

Think about it: - Text: You give it a topic, and it writes a blog post or script. - Images: You type a description, and it paints a masterpiece. - Music: You describe a mood, and it composes a song. - Video: You write a story, and it animates a movie.

This is not science fiction. This is happening right now, and you are about to learn how to control it."
