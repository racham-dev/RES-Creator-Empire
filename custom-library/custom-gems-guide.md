How to Deploy These Gems (For Your Students)

Platform How to Set It Up

# Google Gemini (Gems) 
1. Open Gemini → Click "Create a Gem." 
2. Give it a name (e.g., "Business Navigator").
 3. Paste the instructions. 4. Click "Save" and share the link with your students.

# ChatGPT (Custom GPTs) 
1. Open ChatGPT → Click "Explore GPTs." 
2. Click "Create a GPT."
3. Paste the instructions in the "Instructions" box. 4. Click "Update" → "Save" → "Share via link."
Claude (Projects) 
1. Open Claude → Click "Projects." 
2. Create a new Project. 
3. Paste the instructions in the "Project Instructions" field.
