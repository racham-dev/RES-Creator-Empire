**Tool:** ChatGPT + Claude
**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to outline and write chapters for eBooks and guides.

---

## 1. Tool Setup

- **Claude:** Open `claude.ai`.

---

## 2. How to Outline a Book

A good book outline has:

| Part | Example |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Title** | How to Survive University |
| **Chapter 1** | Introduction: Why University is Hard |
| **Chapter 2** | Time Management Tips |
| **Chapter 3** | How to Study Effectively |
| **Chapter 4** | Building a Network |
| **Chapter 5** | Conclusion: Your Future Starts Now |

**Prompt Formula:**
> *"Create a 10-chapter outline for an eBook titled '[Title]'. Target audience: [Audience]. Tone: [Motivational/Professional]."*

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open ChatGPT.
2. Type this prompt:
   > *"Create a 10-chapter outline for an eBook titled 'How to Survive University'. Target audience: Nigerian university freshers. Tone: Motivational and practical."*
3. Press Enter.
4. Review the outline.
5. Pick one chapter and ask ChatGPT to write it in full.
