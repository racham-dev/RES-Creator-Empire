## 2. The AIDA Framework

AIDA is the most powerful copywriting formula:

| Letter | Meaning | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A** | Attention (Grab their eyes) | "Are you tired of boring content?" |
| **I** | Interest (Keep them reading) | "AI can write your captions in 10 seconds." |
| **D** | Desire (Make them want it) | "Imagine doubling your engagement." |
| **A** | Action (Tell them what to do) | "Click the link to start." |

**Prompt Formula:**
> *"Write an Instagram caption using the AIDA framework for [product/topic]. Tone: [exciting/professional/fun]. Target audience: [university students/business owners]."*

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open ChatGPT.
2. Type this prompt:
   > *"Write an Instagram caption for a new Nigerian sneaker brand using the AIDA framework. Tone: Exciting. Target: University students. Use emojis. Include a Call to Action to visit the website."*
3. Press Enter.
4. Read the output and improve it together.
