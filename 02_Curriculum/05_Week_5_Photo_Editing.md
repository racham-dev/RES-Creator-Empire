# WEEK 5: AI PhotoShoot & Editing

**Tool:** Photoroom or Canva Magic Studio
**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to edit photos, remove backgrounds, change outfits, and upscale images.

---

## 1. Tool Setup

- **Photoroom:**
  1. Go to `photoroom.com`.
  2. Sign up with your email.
  3. Upload a photo to start editing.

- **Canva Magic Studio:**
  1. Go to `canva.com`.
  2. Sign up and open a new design.
  3. Select "Magic Studio" for AI editing tools.

---

## 2. Key Editing Skills

| Skill | Description | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Background Removal** | Remove any background with one click. | Remove a messy room from your selfie. |
| **Outfit Changing** | Change clothing colors or styles. | Turn a white shirt into a blue suit. |
| **Upscaling** | Improve low-quality images. | Turn a blurry photo into HD. |

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open Photoroom or Canva.
2. Upload a selfie.
3. Remove the background.
4. Place yourself in a CEO office background.
5. Add a professional filter.
6. Download the image.

---

## 4. Assignment (Homework)

**Task:** Take a selfie, remove the background, and place yourself in a **CEO office setting**.

- Make it look professional and realistic.
- Use Photoroom or Canva.

**Submit:**
1. The edited image.
2. Your name.

**Deadline:** Friday, 11:59 PM WAT.
