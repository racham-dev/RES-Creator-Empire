# WEEK 1: AI Image Generation

**Tool:** Leonardo.ai (Free) or Bing Image Creator (Free)
**Learning Objective:** Students will learn how to write specific, high-quality prompts to generate professional AI images.

---

## 1. Tool Setup

- **Leonardo.ai:**
  1. Go to `leonardo.ai`.
  2. Sign up with your email (free credits are given daily).
  3. Familiarize yourself with the interface: Prompt box, Generate button, Aspect ratio settings.

- **Bing Image Creator:**
  1. Go to `bing.com/create`.
  2. Sign in with a Microsoft account.
  3. Type your prompt and press "Create."

---

## 2. The Art of Prompt Engineering

A great prompt has 4 parts:

| Part | Example |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Subject** | A 3D character of a cool blue dinosaur |
| **Style/Color** | Wearing sunglasses, neon blue and purple color scheme |
| **Lighting** | Cinematic lighting, neon city background |
| **Quality** | 4K, high resolution, photorealistic |

**Formula:** 
> *"[Subject], [style/color], [lighting], [background], [quality]"*

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Exercise (Live Class)

1. Open Leonardo.ai or Bing Image Creator.
2. Type this prompt:
   > *"3D character of a cool blue dinosaur wearing sunglasses, neon city background, cinematic lighting, 4K."*
3. Press Generate.
4. Wait 10–15 seconds.
5. Download the image.

- It can be a futuristic robot, a cartoon mascot, or a fantasy creature.
- The character must look like it belongs on a product packaging.

**Submit:**

2. The exact prompt you used.
