## 📂 Full 8-Week Curriculum

### Week 1: AI Image Generation
- **Tool:** Leonardo.ai (Free) or Bing Image Creator
- **Skill:** Prompt Engineering (Subject + Style + Lighting + Quality)
- **Assignment:** Create a 3D character for a fictional brand of your choice.
- **Outcome:** You will create your first professional AI image.

---

### Week 2: AI Music & Sound Design
- **Tool:** Suno AI or Udio
- **Skill:** Generating background scores, jingles, and custom audio for businesses.
- **Assignment:** Create a 15-second jingle for a fictional restaurant or bakery.
- **Outcome:** You will understand how to generate custom audio for brands.

---

### Week 3: AI Video Creation
- **Tool:** RunwayML or Pika Labs
- **Skill:** Text-to-video and image-to-video generation.
- **Assignment:** Turn your Week 1 character into a 5-second dancing video.
- **Outcome:** You will create short, engaging video content for social media.

---

### Week 4: AI Copywriting (Ads, Blogs, Captions)
- **Tool:** ChatGPT Plus (covered in Advanced Package)
- **Skill:** The AIDA Framework (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
- **Assignment:** Write a sales pitch for a product you hate (to prove you can sell anything).
- **Outcome:** You will write persuasive copy that converts readers into buyers.

---

### Week 5: AI PhotoShoot & Editing
- **Tool:** Photoroom or Canva Magic Studio
- **Skill:** Background removal, outfit changing, and image upscaling.
- **Assignment:** Take a selfie, remove the background, and place yourself in a CEO office setting.
- **Outcome:** You will edit professional-looking product and portrait images.

---

### Week 6: AI Influencer Avatars
- **Tool:** D-ID or HeyGen
- **Skill:** Creating talking avatars using a single photo and a voiceover.
- **Assignment:** Create a 30-second "faceless" Instagram Reel explaining a topic you love.
- **Outcome:** You will produce AI-generated video content for social media.

---

### Week 7: Book Copywriting (Outlining + Chapters)
- **Tool:** ChatGPT + Claude
- **Skill:** Structuring eBooks, writing introductions, and creating chapter summaries.
- **Assignment:** Outline a 10-chapter guide on "How to Survive University" or "How to Grow Your Business with AI."
- **Outcome:** You will learn to structure long-form content for books, guides, and courses.

---

### Week 8: Portfolio Building + Client Pitching
- **Tool:** Canva
- **Skill:** Creating a 1-page "OneSheet" portfolio and sending cold emails to real businesses.
- **Assignment:** Send a pitch to one real local business.
- **Outcome:** You will have a professional portfolio and know exactly how to land your first client.

## 🤝 Paid Gig Placement Process (Top 30%)

At the end of Week 8, we select the top 30% of graduates based on:
- Consistency (homework submitted every week)
- Prompt quality and creativity
- Coachability (ability to take feedback and improve)

**The Process:**
1. **Selection:** I review all 8 weeks of submissions and select the top 30%.
2. **Portfolio Deck:** I build a professional 10-slide deck showcasing the best work of the selected graduates.
3. **Outreach:** I personally pitch this deck to 50+ SMEs (businesses, brands, agencies) in Nigeria via email and WhatsApp.
4. **Matching:** When an SME says "Yes," we pair them with the selected graduate.
5. **Commission:** RES_Empire takes a one-time **10% commission** on the first gig only. The graduate keeps 90%.

**Protection:** Both parties sign a non-circumvention agreement to ensure fair treatment.
