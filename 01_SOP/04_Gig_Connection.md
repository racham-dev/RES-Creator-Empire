# GIG CONNECTION SOP (Week 8)

**Objective:** To connect the top 30% of graduates to SMEs, secure paid gigs, and collect the 10% commission on the first gig only.

---

## Step 1: Select the Top 30% (Week 8 – Monday)

- **Criteria:**
  - Consistency (submitted homework every week).
  - Prompt quality (specific, creative, high-resolution outputs).
  - Coachability (took feedback and improved).
- **Math:** 60 students × 30% = **18 students**.
- **Action:** Create a WhatsApp broadcast list with these 18 students.
- **Message:** *"Congratulations! You have been selected as part of the top 30% of RES Creators Cohort 1. You are now eligible for paid gigs with SMEs. I will be reaching out to businesses this week. Stay ready."*

---

## Step 2: Build the Portfolio Deck (Week 8 – Tuesday)

- **Tool:** Canva or Google Slides.
- **Slides:**
  1. **Title Slide:** "RES Creators – Top AI Talent for Your Business"
  2. **Slide 2:** "Who We Are" (1 sentence: *"We train Nigerian university freshers in AI and connect them to SMEs."*)
  3. **Slide 3–11:** Show 1 project per student (18 students = 9 slides with 2 images each).
  4. **Slide 12:** "Why Hire Us?" (Affordable, Fast, AI-Powered, Nigerian-based).
  5. **Slide 13:** Call to Action: "Book a 5-minute discovery call. [Your Email] [Your WhatsApp]"
- **Save as PDF:** `RES_Creators_Top_Talent.pdf`

---

## Step 3: Outreach to 50 SMEs (Week 8 – Wednesday)

- **Target:** Local businesses, restaurants, fashion brands, agencies, and startups in Nigeria.
- **Platform:** Email and WhatsApp.
- **Script (Email):**
  > *Subject: AI Creators for Your Business – Affordable & Ready*
  > 
  > *Dear [Business Owner],*
  > 
  > *My name is [Your Name], founder of RES Creators. I train Nigerian university freshers in AI skills – image generation, video creation, copywriting, and music production.*
  > 
  > *I have a pool of 18 top-tier AI creators who are ready to help your business with:*
  > - *Social media content (images & videos)*
  > - *Ad copy and blog posts*
  > - *Product photography editing*
  > - *Brand jingles and sound design*
  > 
  > *They are professional, fast, and affordable. I have attached a portfolio deck of their best work.*
  > 
  > *Would you be open to a 5-minute call this week? I can show you exactly how they can help your business grow.*
  > 
  > *Warm regards,*
  > *Emmanuel Racham*
  > *RES Creators*
  > *+2348168481995*

---

## Step 4: The Contract (When an SME Says Yes)

- **Action:** Send the SME an invoice.
- **Action:** Send the student a Non-Circumvention Agreement.
- **Agreement Terms:**
  1. Student agrees to pay RES Creators 10% of the first payment from this client.
  2. Student agrees not to take this client off-platform (i.e., work directly for them) for 6 months.
  3. RES Creators will handle invoicing and payment collection for the first gig.

---

## Step 5: Payment Processing

- SME pays you via bank transfer.
- You take 10% (e.g., if gig is ₦50,000, you keep ₦5,000).
- You send the student ₦45,000 via bank transfer **within 24 hours**.
- Send the student a WhatsApp message: *"Payment sent. Congratulations on your first gig! You're officially a freelancer. Keep building."*
