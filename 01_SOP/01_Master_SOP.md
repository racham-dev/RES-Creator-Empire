## SECTION C: LIVE COHORT SOP (Saturdays & Sundays)

### Saturday: The Live Class (Google Meet)
- **Time:** 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM WAT.
- **Zoom/Meet Link:** Create a recurring link. Send it to WhatsApp group at 8:00 AM.

**The 2-Hour Class Flow:**
- **10:00 – 10:15 (Check-in):** Screen share the "Student Playbook." Say: *"Welcome, Creators! Let's do a quick roll call. Type 'Present' in the chat. And drop one word that describes how you feel about AI right now."*
- **10:15 – 10:45 (The Teach):** Screen share your tool (e.g., Leonardo.ai). Walk them through the interface. Do not assume they know what "prompt" means. Show them exactly where to type it.
- **10:45 – 11:30 (The Live Challenge):** Give them a specific task. *"I want you to create a picture of a Nigerian village at sunset. Post it in the WhatsApp group within 20 minutes."* While they work, stay silent. Keep your video on so they feel your presence.
- **11:30 – 12:00 (Q&A & Homework):** Pick 3 submissions from WhatsApp (if they've posted) and critique them live on screen. Give homework: *"Your homework for this week is to create [Task]. Post it in the WhatsApp group by Friday. Sunday, we review everyone's work."* Stop the recording here. Upload to YouTube and Telegram by 4:00 PM Saturday.

### Sunday: The Feedback & Community Day (WhatsApp Group)
- **Time:** 4:00 PM – 5:00 PM WAT.
- **Format:** Voice notes and text.

**The Flow:**
- **4:00 PM:** *"Good afternoon Creators! Let's review your homework from Saturday. I saw [Number] submissions."*
- **4:05 PM – 4:30 PM (Praise & Highlight):** Screenshot 3 excellent submissions. Post them. Say: *"Look at [Name]'s work. The lighting is perfect. Why did you do that, [Name]? Tell us your secret."* (This encourages peer learning).
- **4:30 PM – 4:45 PM (Critique):** Pick 3 that need work. Use text, not voice, so they can read and re-read. Say: *"I noticed many of you used generic prompts like 'a beautiful car.' That's boring. Next time, try 'a 2024 black sports car, neon lights, rain on windshield.' Be specific."*
- **4:45 PM – 5:00 PM (Week Preview):** *"Next Saturday, we are doing AI Video. Start thinking about what you want to create. I'm going to challenge you to create a 5-second ad for a product you love."*

**WELCOME TO RES CREATORS – CLASS OF 2026!**  

Here are the 4 rules for our 8 weeks together. Follow them, and you will succeed.

1. **The 24-Hour Rule:** All homework must be posted in the WhatsApp group within 24 hours of the class ending. No excuses.
2. **The "Help a Buddy" Rule:** If you see someone struggling with a prompt, help them. We rise together. 
3. **The "No Spam" Rule:** This group is for AI work and class announcements only. No funny videos, memes, or gossip.
4. **The "Gig Ready" Rule:** The top 30% of graduates get gigs. I will be tracking your submissions every week. Be consistent.
