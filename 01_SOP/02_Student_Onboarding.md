# STUDENT ONBOARDING SOP

**Objective:** To ensure every paying student feels welcomed, informed, and ready for Week 1 within 5 minutes of payment.

---

## Step 1: Confirm Payment
- Receive bank alert (₦6,000 for Starter, ₦15,000 for Advanced).
- Send a DM to the student: *"Payment confirmed! Welcome to RES Creators. I'm adding you to the WhatsApp group right now."*

---

## Step 2: Add to WhatsApp Group
- Add the student to the WhatsApp group: `RES Creators – Cohort 1 (July 2026)`.
- Immediately send a DM: *"You're in! Please PIN the first 3 messages in the group. They contain your class schedule, rules, and all materials."*

---

## Step 3: Send Google Drive Access
- Send the Google Drive link: `[Insert Link]`.
- Say: *"This folder contains your Student Playbook, Week-by-week guides, and the Certificate Template. Download the Playbook before Saturday's class."*

---

## Step 4: Send Welcome Video (Script)
- Send a 60-second voice note or video with this exact script:

> *"Hey [Name]! Welcome to RES Creators. You've just taken the first step to becoming an AI freelancer. Over the next 8 weeks, you're going to learn how to create images, music, videos, and copy that businesses pay for. 
> 
> Here is what you need to do right now:
> 1. Pin the first 3 messages in the WhatsApp group.
> 2. Download the Student Playbook from Google Drive.
> 3. Show up this Saturday at 10:00 AM WAT on Google Meet.
> 
> You are not just a student. You are a Creator. Let's build your future. See you Saturday!"*

---

## Step 5: Send Class Rules (As a text message)
- Copy and paste the Class Rules from `01_SOP/01_Master_SOP.md` (Section D) and send it as a separate message.
- Say: *"Read these once. They keep this community strong."*

---

## Step 6: Set Expectations
- Send this final message: *"Your homework will start after Saturday's class. For now, just rest, set your alarm for 9:30 AM on Saturday, and come ready to learn. You belong here."*
