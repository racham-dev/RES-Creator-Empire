# CLASS DELIVERY SOP (Saturday Live Classes)

**Objective:** To deliver a 2-hour live class that is engaging, practical, and leaves students with a clear assignment.

---

## 10:00 AM – 10:15 AM: Roll Call & Energy

- **Action:** Share screen. Open the Google Meet chat.
- **Script:**
  > *"Good morning, Creators! Welcome to Week [X] of RES Creators. Let's do a roll call. Type 'Present' in the chat right now. Also, drop one word that describes how you feel about AI today – don't overthink it, just one word."*
- **Wait 2 minutes for responses.**
- **Response:** *"I see 20 'Present's and words like 'Excited,' 'Nervous,' and 'Ready.' That's perfect. Let's turn that nervous energy into skill."*

---

## 10:15 AM – 10:45 AM: The Teach (Tool Demonstration)

- **Action:** Share your screen. Open the tool for the week (e.g., Leonardo.ai).
- **Script:**
  > *"Today, we are mastering [Topic]. Watch my screen carefully. I'm going to show you exactly how to use [Tool Name]. 
  > 
  > First, open your browser. Go to [URL]. Sign up with your email. 
  > 
  > Now, look at the prompt box. This is where the magic happens. A prompt is just a description of what you want. The more specific you are, the better the result.
  > 
  > Watch this: I'm going to type '3D character of a cool blue dinosaur wearing sunglasses, neon city background, cinematic lighting, 4K.' 
  > 
  > Press Generate. Wait 10 seconds. Look at that. You just created art that would cost ₦20,000 from a graphic designer."*
- **Do this 3 times with 3 different examples.**

---

## 10:45 AM – 11:30 AM: The Live Challenge (Practice Time)

- **Action:** Stop screen sharing. Turn your camera on fully.
- **Script:**
  > *"Okay, your turn. I want you to create something right now. Here is your challenge: 
  > 
  > [Insert Challenge, e.g., 'Create a picture of a Nigerian village at sunset.']
  > 
  > You have 20 minutes. Post your result in the WhatsApp group. I will pick the top 3 and feature them on our Instagram page. Go!"*
- **Rule:** While they work, do not talk. Stay silent with your camera on. This builds focus and urgency.

---

## 11:30 AM – 12:00 PM: Q&A & Homework Assignment

- **Action:** Open WhatsApp on your screen. Show 3 submissions.
- **Script (Praise):** *"Look at [Name]'s work. The lighting is perfect. Why did you do that, [Name]? Tell us your secret."*
- **Script (Critique):** *"I noticed many of you used generic prompts like 'a beautiful car.' That's boring. Next time, try 'a 2024 black sports car, neon lights, rain on windshield.' Be specific."*
- **Script (Homework):**
  > *"Here is your homework for this week: 
  > 
  > [Insert Assignment, e.g., 'Create a 3D character for a fictional brand of your choice.']
  > 
  > Post it in the WhatsApp group by Friday, 11:59 PM WAT. Sunday, we review everyone's work. 
  > 
  > Thank you for showing up, Creators. You are one week closer to earning with AI. See you Sunday at 4 PM."*
- **Stop Recording.**

---

## Post-Class Action (By 4:00 PM Saturday)

1. Upload the recording to YouTube.
2. Upload the recording to Telegram Channel.
3. Post the homework assignment as a pinned message in WhatsApp.
4. Post the recording links in Telegram with the message: *"Week [X] recording is live. Catch up if you missed it."*
