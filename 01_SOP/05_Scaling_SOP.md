# SCALING SOP (For Cohort 2 and Beyond)

**Objective:** To use Cohort 1's success to launch Cohort 2 with minimal effort and maximum enrollment.

---

## Phase 1: Alumni Referral Program (Day 1 After Graduation)

- **Action:** Send a WhatsApp broadcast to all 60 Cohort 1 graduates.
- **Message:**
  > *"Congratulations, Creators! You are now alumni of RES Creators. 
  > 
  > Here is how you can earn from this: 
  > For every friend you refer to Cohort 2 who enrolls, you get 10% of their fee as a cash commission. 
  > 
  > That means if you refer 3 students, you earn ₦1,800 (₦600 each). If you refer 10, you earn ₦6,000. 
  > 
  > Send them my contact. Let's build this community together."*

---

## Phase 2: Telegram Gig Board (For Alumni Only)

- **Action:** Create a private Telegram group: `RES Creators – Alumni Gig Board`.
- **Description:** *"Paid gigs for RES Creators alumni. Only verified SMEs post here. No spam. Submit your best work."*
- **Posting Format for Gig Alerts:**
  > *📢 GIG ALERT*
  > *Client: [Business Name]*
  > *Task: [e.g., 10 Instagram captions for a fashion brand]*
  > *Pay: ₦7,000*
  > *Deadline: [Date]*
  > *Submit your portfolio to: [DM or Email]*
- **Rule:** You post these gigs weekly. This keeps alumni engaged and makes them want to refer friends.

---

## Phase 3: Social Media Announcement for Cohort 2

- **Action:** Post a 1-minute video on Instagram and TikTok.
- **Script:**
  > *"60 university freshers just graduated from RES Creators. They walked in knowing nothing about AI. They walked out as freelancers earning money. 
  > 
  > Cohort 2 opens in 2 weeks. Only 60 seats again. Price is still ₦6,000 for 8 weeks. 
  > 
  > If you are a university student who wants to learn AI and get paid, DM me the word 'AI' right now. The waitlist is filling up fast."*
- **Caption:** *"Cohort 1 is done. Cohort 2 is next. DM 'AI' to reserve your spot. Let's build your future."*

---

## Phase 4: Internal Operations Review (Before Cohort 2)

- **Action:** Review the following before launching Cohort 2:
  - **Curriculum:** Which weeks were hardest to teach? (Adjust content).
  - **Tools:** Did any tool fail? (Find alternatives).
  - **Homework:** Were students overwhelmed? (Reduce workload).
  - **WhatsApp/Telegram:** Are the pinned messages still effective? (Update).
- **Action:** Update the GitHub repo with all changes before announcing Cohort 2.
